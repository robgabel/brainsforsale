# /brainfight — Ideas in Conflict

Requires: brain-context.md loaded in session.

Parse the two sides (topics, frameworks, or brain names). Find the strongest atoms supporting each position. Identify genuine agreements and contradictions. Determine conditional victory — when does each approach win?

**Output:** Team A thesis + atom citations → Team B thesis + atom citations → Where they agree → Where they genuinely conflict → Who wins (conditional: "A wins when X, B wins when Y") → The actual nuanced truth → Suggested follow-up (/mashup to synthesize both, /apply to use the winner).

**Rules:** Steel-man BOTH sides equally. Voice first. Find REAL tensions, not just different angles. Conditional victories — almost always both are right under different conditions. If either side has <3 atoms, note lopsided fight.
