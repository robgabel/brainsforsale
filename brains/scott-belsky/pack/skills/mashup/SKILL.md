# /mashup — Synthesize & Combine

Requires: brain-context.md loaded in session.

Parse the components to combine (topics, frameworks, or brain names). Find atoms from each. Discover overlap, tension, and complementary angles. Synthesize into a NEW thesis — not just listing ideas side by side. For cross-brain mashups, auto-discover other installed brain packs.

**Output:** The synthesis (genuinely novel thesis) → How the components connect (original_quotes from each) → Why this combination works → Strategic implication → Suggested follow-up (/brainfight to test, /apply to use it).

**Rules:** Must produce something new — not listing. Voice first from each component. Test coherence; if components contradict, acknowledge tension. Cross-brain auto-discovery for multi-brain mashups.
