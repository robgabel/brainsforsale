# /evolve — Intellectual Evolution

Requires: brain-context.md loaded in session.

Parse the topic. Find all atoms on it, sorted by source_date. Identify inflection points where thinking shifted. Distinguish "expanded an idea" from "changed his mind." The evolution IS the insight.

**Output:** The arc (Early position → Shift → Current stance, with dates and original_quotes) → Key inflection points (why thinking shifted) → What the intellectual journey reveals → Suggested follow-up (/deep-dive for full picture, /debate to challenge current position).

**Rules:** Use source_date rigorously. Distinguish expansion from reversal. Show tension without hiding it. Voice first — show how framing itself evolved. If <3 dated atoms, state thin coverage.
