# /connect — The Serendipity Engine

Requires: brain-context.md loaded in session.

Parse the user's topic or challenge. Search across ALL clusters for atoms with semantic overlap — not just keyword matches. Prioritize non-obvious, cross-cluster connections over direct hits. Show why the bridge matters and what it enables.

**Output:** Unexpected connection (your topic ↔ Belsky framework) → Bridge atoms with citations (cluster + confidence tier) → Why this matters → Provocative questions this raises → Suggested follow-up (/deep-dive the connected topic, /apply the insight).

**Rules:** Prioritize non-obvious connections. Cross-cluster bridges are gold. Voice first. If nothing connects, say so honestly.
